### We need your help in translating Rishav Music Bot. How to Contribute?

You can edit [`en.yml`](https://github.com/TeamRishav/RishavMusicBot/blob/master/strings/langs/en.yml) present in langs folder to your own language and send us the edited file at [@RishavSupport](https://t.me/RishavSupport)

- > Points to remember while editing : <br> - Make sure you dont change any `{0}` or `{1}` while editing your codes <br> - Don’t change "general_1" or any other such keys present.
